Thrust-to-Weight Ratio​

For an aircraft or rocket to accelerate upward, the thrust-to-weight ratio must exceed unity:

TWR=Fthrustm g0>1\text{TWR} = \frac{F_{thrust}}{m \, g_0} > 1TWR=mg0​Fthrust​​>1
